PRIEST

The Venerable Dr. Gideon Uzomechina is a spiritual man whose love for the Lord Jesus Christ and his mission to the world has no boundaries. He is an ordained pastor of the Anglican Communion (Anglo-Catholic Church) since 2002 following his seminary training. Being very passionate in his pastoral responsibilities and actively engaged in the community, Gideon brings a message of Christâ€™s love and inclusiveness to his members and to the needy and unreached. Many who have heard him deliver a sermon for the first time, concur that he has been blessed with the gifts of preaching and teaching the Word and Sacrament. His spiritual knowledge was enhanced by studying the life, time, and ministry of Jesus Christ in the land of Palestine in ecumenical and interfaith settings at St. Georges College and Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Mount Scopus, Jerusalem, Israel in 2008 His studies in Israel also included many fact-finding missions to places such as Egypt, Jordan, Syria, Golan Heights and Istanbul, Turkey. Archdeacon Gideon is a trained conflict management and resolution specialist. Following a tumultuous and trying period of his life, God led him alongside Pastor Colin Baptiste and Pastor Harry Watson Jr. into formation and planting of the Anglican church of the Messiah (Anglican Communion), Plainfield, New Jersey where he is the Archdeacon and Vicar.
